# Aarogya Sahayak

## Your AI-Powered Digital Health Companion

Aarogya Sahayak is a modern healthcare platform focused on simplifying health management through AI-powered insights, digital medical records, multilingual accessibility, and seamless patient support.

Built with a mobile-first approach, the platform helps users securely manage medical records, understand health reports in simple language, and connect healthcare workflows in one unified system.

## Features

- AI-powered medical report analysis
- Family health record management
- Multilingual and mobile-first experience
- Personalized health insights
- Appointment and consultation workflows
- Prescription and pharmacy integration
- Secure digital health data management
- Responsive UI for all devices
- Real-time video consultation support
- Smart healthcare dashboards

---

## Problem Statement

Managing healthcare records and understanding medical reports can be difficult, especially for:
- Migrant workers
- Rural communities
- Elderly patients
- Non-English speakers

Aarogya Sahayak aims to bridge this gap by making healthcare:
- Accessible
- Understandable
- Secure
- AI-assisted
